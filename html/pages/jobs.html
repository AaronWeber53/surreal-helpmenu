Trucking

Setup

For any tier of this job you can either bring your own vehicle or rent one. If you choose to rent one go to the lower docks at zip (39) to retrieve it. If you choose to use your own vehicle you need to register your vehicle by going in the radial menu Job > Register Vehicle.
Tier 1 (Van)
- Within your radial menu select "Toggle Deliveries" which will begin to give you waypoints in which to deliver to. - When you reach your destination you must grab packages out of your van and then bring them to the desired spot for the delivery. - After finishing a shipment your payslip will begin to fill out. To collect your money return to the docks and interact with the computer.
(WIP) Tier 2 (Business)
This will be implemented when player businesses are implemented
Tier 3 (Semi-Truck)
- When you register or rent a semi truck you may use your radial menu and "Request Trailer". - These jobs only allow one person per request so if there are no avaliable requests left it will tell you and you must wait till you can get another delivery. - When you get a delivery request you must go to the location of the trailer and hook it up. Then you will drive the trailer to the destination which you will drop it off and collect your money.
