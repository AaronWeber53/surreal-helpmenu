General World Information

Car Shops

PLEASE NOTE

Any imported vehicles within shops may take a couple of seconds or more to load if you have never seen it before so please be patient when looking through our list.

Vanilla Shop

This shop can be located at zip (200). This shop holds any base game vehicles

Import Shop

This shop can be located at zip (656) and requires a drivers license to enter. This shop holds any of our imported cars.

Motorcycle Shop

This shop can be located at zip (213). This shop holds any of our imported and base game Motorcycles.

Boat Shop

This shop is located at zip (356) at the marina. This shop holds any boats that we have avaliable within our city

Race Shop

This shop is located at zip (561). This shop requires a car salesman to be present and also requires you to have a racing license to purchase any car.
Blips of Interest
Blips of interest. like shops, gun stores, houses, etc.
  • - Hospital, where to heal or location that you will be revived.
  • - City hall, can change jobs here and purchase licenses.
  • - Garage, where you store cars.
  • - Depot/Impound where to find your car if lost and not within a garage.
City Hall
City halls are used to switch between any of the players jobs and also can be used to purchase licenses.
Hospital
Each hospital will have a place to checkin and/or lay in a hospital bed to heal yourself. If you bleed out you will respawn to the closest hospital location.
Depot/Impound
Impounds will hold your vehicle if you ever lose it without the vehicle being located within a garage.
